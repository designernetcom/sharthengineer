Sharth Engineers

Sharth Engineers

Since our Inception in 2015, we have been dedicated to delivering high-quality sheet metal components and Heavy Fabricated products. Situated in the heart of Chakan MIDC, Pune, India our facility boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, all consolidated under one roof.
As proud vendors to esteemed companies such as Wirtgen India, Bomag, Ziehl-Abegg, Putzmeister, and Bharat Forge, we have established ourselves as reliable partners in the industry.
Moreover, our commitment to quality is underscored by certifications from reputable bodies such as TUV. We hold certifications for ISO 3834, EN15085, and AS9001D, reflecting our adherence to stringent quality standards.
Here's a brief overview of our facility:
1. Laser Cutting Setup: Equipped with 2 Trumpf machines, we ensure precision and efficiency in our laser cutting operations.
2. Turret Punching Machine: Utilizing Trumpf technology, our turret punching machine enables fast and accurate punching of sheet metal components.
3. Bending Machines: We employ top-of-the-line machines from Ermakson and Yawaei for bending operations, ensuring consistency and accuracy.
4. Fabrication Setup: Our fabrication facility is equipped with advanced machinery and skilled manpower to handle heavy fabrication projects efficiently.
5. Powder Coating: We offer powder coating services to enhance the durability and aesthetics of our products.
6. Shot Blasting: Our shot blasting facility ensures thorough surface preparation, essential for achieving optimal coating adhesion and surface finish.
7. Liquid Painting: With state-of-the-art painting equipment, we provide high-quality liquid painting services to meet diverse customer requirements.
